BaconBomber Ranger Hunter Build

Hi, i'm BaconBomber
Guild Lead of Phantom Knigts and i have been playing since Mod 14.
I have created this build from different sources, but mostly using Discord server "The Wolf Pack" and using the Rainer tool to build characters to find the right stats and also using this Excel Sheet to see information about everything in the game.

Rotation(s)

I have one main rotation i go for

Single target

  1. Disruptive Shot (daily) + 10% dmg for 5 seconds
  2. Constricting Arrow(Encounter, Strong grasping roots)
  3. Longstrider Shot(Encounter)
  4. Hindering Shot(Encounter, Weak grasping roots)
  5. Aimed Shot(At-Will)
  6. Disruptive Shot (daily) + 10% dmg for 5 seconds
  7. Hindering Shot(Encounter, Weak grasping roots)
  8. Aimed Shot(At-Will)
  9. Hindering Shot(Encounter, Weak grasping roots)

Larger Mob groups

  1. Disruptive Shot (daily) + 10% dmg for 5 seconds
  2. Constricting Arrow(Encounter, Strong grasping roots)
  3. Longstrider Shot(Encounter)
  4. Hindering Shot(Encounter, Weak grasping roots)
  5. Aimed Shot(At-Will)
  6. Shift to Meleestance
  7. Go to middle of group and use
  8. Steel Breeze(Encounter, 10% Stamina regeneration)
  9. Hindering Strike(Encounter, Strong Grasping Root)
  10. Shift to Ranged stance
  11. REPEAT

Boons

We go from Tier 1 to the Master Boons.

At this moment (2021-04-18 or 18-04-2021) Sharandar still have a few boons left that i will add in time. From what i can see i will put the last boons into HP

Tier 1 Boons

5 for Power / 5 for Critical Strike / 5 for HP

Tier 2 Boons

5 for Power / 5 for Armor Penetration / 5 for Defense / 5 for HP / 5 for Movement Speed

Tier 3 Boons

5 for Power / 5 for Combat Advantage / 5 for Awareness / 4 for HP

Tier 4 Boons

5 for Power / 5 for Accuracy

Tier 5 Boons

4 for Forte / 4 for Action Point Gain

Master Boons

3 for Blood Lust for the Rank 2 improvement. (Change on Enounter use to do the following: Rank 2: Do an additional attack equal to 120% of your weapon damage plus 10% per rank)

Guild Boons

Offence: go for Critical Severity boon

Defence: go for Defence boon

Utility: I have the mount speed boon, but if you can go for the Revive Sickness boon

Enchantments

  • 5 utility - Dark Enchantments
  • 5 Defence - Azure Encahntments
  • 9 offense - 8 Tactical Enchantments + 1 Assassin's Enchantment
